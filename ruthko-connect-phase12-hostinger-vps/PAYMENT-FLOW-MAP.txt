1. Visitor opens index.html.
2. Visitor clicks Sponsor Portal.
3. Visitor selects a package.
4. Visitor lands on payments.html.
5. If Stripe link exists, buyer goes to Stripe checkout.
6. If Stripe link is blank, buyer goes to intake.html.
7. Netlify Forms captures the lead.
8. Ruthko follows up by email, phone, or invoice.
