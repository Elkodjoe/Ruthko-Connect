RUTHKO CONNECT LEAD FLOW MAP

Employer lead flow:
Employer submits staffing request.
Netlify captures form.
Ruthko reviews need.
Lead moves to CRM.
Follow-up email goes out.
Discovery call is scheduled.
Contract or staffing agreement is sent.

Candidate lead flow:
Candidate submits interest.
Ruthko reviews profession, country, and visa interest.
Candidate is grouped by profession.
Candidate receives next-step email.
Candidate uploads resume and credentials later.

Sponsor lead flow:
Sponsor selects package.
Ruthko reviews package and goals.
Sponsor receives proposal.
Stripe payment link is sent.
Sponsor is added to sponsor tracker.

Vendor lead flow:
Vendor submits booth request.
Ruthko reviews product or service.
Vendor receives booth package.
Stripe payment link is sent.
Vendor is added to booth tracker.

Event lead flow:
Attendee submits RSVP.
Ruthko reviews attendee type.
Attendee receives event details.
Attendee is added to email list.

Best tools for next automation:
Netlify Forms for capture.
Zapier or Make for automation.
Google Sheets or Airtable for tracking.
Stripe for payment.
Resend or Gmail for email confirmation.
