Ruthko Connect Reports Flow

1. Public forms collect employer, candidate, sponsor, vendor, event, and partner interest.
2. CRM stores lead records and stages.
3. Tasks track follow-up work.
4. Payments store paid sponsor, vendor, booth, and ticket activity.
5. Event Operations stores speakers, sponsors, vendors, attendees, and run sheet details.
6. Reports reads the data and produces:
   - KPI cards
   - monthly chart
   - lead mix
   - event report
   - sponsor and vendor report
   - campaign report
   - action alerts
   - executive brief
   - CSV export

Reports page is admin-only.
Public site stays clean.
