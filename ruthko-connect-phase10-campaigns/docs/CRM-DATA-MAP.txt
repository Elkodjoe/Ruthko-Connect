Recommended operating flow

1. New form submission enters as New.
2. Ruthko reviews and changes it to Qualified.
3. Proposal or invoice goes out.
4. Payment or signed agreement moves it to Closed Won.
5. Unqualified or declined leads move to Closed Lost.

High-value follow-up rules

Employer lead over $25,000: follow up within 24 hours.
Sponsor lead over $5,000: send proposal same day.
Candidate lead with nurse profession: tag for EB-3 review.
Vendor booth request: send payment link and booth rules.
