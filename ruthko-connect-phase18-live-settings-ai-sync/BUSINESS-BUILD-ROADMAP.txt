RUTHKO CONNECT BUSINESS BUILD ROADMAP

Phase 1: Static launch
Goal: Put Ruthko Connect online.
Features:
- Public dashboard preview
- Staffing request form
- Sponsor packages
- WhatsApp contact
- Events overview

Phase 2: Lead capture
Goal: Collect real business leads.
Features:
- Employer staffing form
- Sponsor interest form
- Vendor booth form
- Candidate interest form
- Google Sheets or Airtable tracking

Phase 3: Payments
Goal: Accept money from sponsors and vendors.
Features:
- Stripe payment links
- Sponsor package checkout
- Vendor booth checkout
- Confirmation email

Phase 4: Admin CRM
Goal: Manage leads and follow-ups.
Features:
- Lead status
- Pipeline stage
- Follow-up reminders
- Sponsor status
- Employer contact history

Phase 5: Full SaaS build
Goal: Move from static site to login-based platform.
Recommended stack:
- React or Next.js frontend
- Supabase database and authentication
- Stripe payments
- Resend email
- Netlify or Vercel deployment
